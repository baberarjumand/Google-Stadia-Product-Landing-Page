One place for all the ways we play

Stadia let's you instantly enjoy games in up to 4K on your TV without a console.
You can also play across laptops, desktops, Pixel phones (select models) with cross-screen early access starting from day one.

4K 60FPS Gaming without even a console

Stadia was formally announced during Google's keynote address at the 2019 Game Developers Conference in March 2019.
Stadia will be powered by powerful systems housed in Google data centers across the globe.
With data centers in more than 200 different countries and more than 7,500 edge nodes around the world,
Google is able to provide high-quality game streaming to practically any device.
All you will need is an internet connection.
